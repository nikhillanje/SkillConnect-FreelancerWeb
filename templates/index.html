Frquently Asked Questions

FAQ

  • How do I create a freelancer/client account?
    You can sign up as a freelancer or client by clicking on the signup button and selecting your role.
  • Is this platform only for Indian users?
    Yes, currently our platform is designed only for Indian freelancers and clients.
  • How are payments handled securely?
    Payments are processed through secure gateways with escrow protection for both clients and freelancers.
  • What service fees are charged?
    A small percentage of the project fee is charged as a service fee. Details are available on our pricing page.
  • How do I contact support?
    You can contact our support team through the “Contact Us” page or email us directly.
